Design Intent

1. Material Selection
	Approved Material: You must use ABS material.
	Technical Reason: The part operates in conditions with temperatures close to 100°C.
	Banned Materials: Do not use PLA. It cannot withstand 100°C and the part will deform and fail.

2. Printing Orientation
	Approved Orientation: Print the structural parts in the Vertical Orientation.
	Technical Reason: Horizontal printing distorts the part and changes the circular shape into an oval shape.
	Result of Failure: An oval shape causes leakage at the bypass-connector interface.

3. Printing Settings:
	All specific printing parameters and details are saved in the Printing Settings File of the project.
